Sara "Red" Alvarado (Willow Red) is an artist, programmer, and designer currently based in Los Angeles, CA. She is a 4th year student at the University of Southern California, pursuing a cross-disciplinary education with a Bachelor's of Art in Art and a minor in Computer Programming.

Sara's use of the pseudonym "Willow Red" (or Red, for short), is more than an online username. It is an embodiment of her interest in online spaces and relationships, virtual personas, and characters. Much of her work is a reflection on self-identity, and utilizing the digital world to create one's own fantasy. With other pieces, she questions her perception of reality, herself, and others.

Sara explores such ideas with a variety of mediums, including drawing, digital illustration, pixel art, and photography. Additionally, with knowledge in both code and art, Sara creates interactive works.
